# SGI O350/Tezro Dell A8 Rail Rack Mount Adapter

## Goal

As the original O350/Tezro rack mount kit is unobtainium, we want to adapt a commonly available Dell A8 style rail to an SGI O350/Tezro.

A cheaper (and likely more sensible) solution is to just sit your SGI on a shelf. Running the SGI on rails provides little benefit, but it's not quite as cool as hacking together a sliding rail.

## Parts

### Drill Guide

The Dell A8 inner rails must be violently adapted to mount to the side of your SGI. 

#### 3D printable files

- Part Studio 1 - FrontJig
  - Guides drilling at what will be the front (closest to SGI faceplate) of the inner rail
- Part Studio 1 - Rear Jig
  - Guides drilling at what will be the rear (closest to the power connector) of the inner rail

#### What you need to bring to the party

- A steel drill guide for 5/16 drill
  - Designed outer diameter is 11mm
  - I used the guide which came with [this](https://www.amazon.ca/dp/B0BZWLGBQD) set. 
  - Might be able to get away with 3D printing a 5/16 drill guide with an 11mm OD if you can't find something handy. I did not include one. 
- A 5/16 drill bit which is steel rated
